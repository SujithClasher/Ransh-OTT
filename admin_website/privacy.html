Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Ransh OTT ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you use the Ransh OTT mobile application and website.

1. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

Ransh OTT is designed for children and families. We take children's privacy very seriously and comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and similar regulations worldwide.

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without verifiable parental consent.
  • We do not display targeted or behavioural advertising to children.
  • We do not sell, share, or rent any personal data of our users to third parties.
  • Content is curated to be age-appropriate for children.

2. Information We Collect

We collect minimal information necessary to provide our service:

  • Account Information: Email address and display name provided during registration via Firebase Authentication (Google Sign-In).
  • Subscription Data: Subscription status, plan type, and payment transaction IDs (processed securely by Razorpay; we do not store credit card or bank details).
  • Device Information: Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token for push notifications, device type (phone/tablet/TV) for UI optimization.
  • Usage Data: View counts on content for analytics and recommendation purposes (aggregated, not personal).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain the Ransh OTT streaming service.
  • To manage your account and subscription status.
  • To send push notifications about new content (you can opt out in device settings).
  • To improve our content library and user experience.
  • To provide customer support.

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted third-party services:

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication, database, cloud messaging, and hosting. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Mux: Video streaming and delivery. Mux Privacy Policy
  • Razorpay: Payment processing (subscription payments only). Razorpay Privacy Policy

5. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is stored securely on Firebase (Google Cloud) servers.
  • Downloaded content is encrypted using AES-256 encryption on the device.
  • We use HTTPS for all network communications.
  • Payment data is handled entirely by Razorpay's PCI-DSS compliant systems.

6. Parental Controls

Ransh OTT includes a parental gate feature that requires solving a simple math puzzle before accessing settings, preventing young children from modifying app settings without parental supervision.

7. Data Retention and Deletion

  • Account data is retained as long as your account is active.
  • You may request deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at the email below.
  • Upon account deletion, all personal data will be removed within 30 days.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Opt out of push notifications via device settings.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes via push notification or in-app notice. The "Last updated" date at the top reflects the most recent revision.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or data practices, please contact us:

Email: support@ranshott.com
